Krystian's Top Movies List

A list of my favourite movies of all time

Bullet Train

Story: 6/10

Visuals: 7/10

Soundtrack: 8/10

Characters: 8/10

A great movie, a lot of action and comedy make it a really fun watch, especially with friends.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

Story: 7/10

Visuals: 7/10

Soundtrack: 10/10

Characters: 9/10

Fourth movie from the "Harry Potter" series. Honestly, not the best one but I've rewatched it recently and the music always makes this movie magical for me.

Captain America: The First Avenger

Story: 7/10

Visuals: 7/10

Soundtrack: 7/10

Characters: 5/10

Only a few interesting characters with the best one being the main character, Steve Rogers. The first Captain America movie and honestly it's a great introduction to that character and it makes for a decent movie overall.
